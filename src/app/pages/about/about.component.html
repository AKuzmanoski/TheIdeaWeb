iDeal World full of ideas and ideal deals.

The idea is the main concept of this system.

Making deals is what this system is all about.

You can be either buyer or seller. That means, you can offer your ideas for sell or you can buy idea from other users of the iDeal community. This is done by protocol for making deals which is secure.

Of course there are many other things that you can do with iDeal:

  • Keep your ideas secure and accessible
  • Find incubators for your ideas
  • Keep track of your problems
  • Share your ideas and problems
  • Search for ideas, problems, users, organizations ...
  • Solve problems from outer users
  • Create organizations
  • Invite people and structure your organization
